# Stock Before/After Images for Industry Pages

Free commercial-use stock photos for all 11 industries. **Free sources prioritized** (Pexels, Unsplash, Pixabay). Paid fallbacks noted only where free options are scarce.

**License quick reference:**
- **Pexels** — Free commercial use, no attribution required
- **Unsplash** — Free commercial use, no attribution required
- **Pixabay** — Free commercial use, no attribution required
- **Vecteezy/Freepik** — Some free, check per image (may require attribution)

**Image specs needed:** 800x600px, WebP, 4:3 aspect ratio, 3 pairs per industry
